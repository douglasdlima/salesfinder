We’re here to connect people with their favourite brands in a rewarding way.

Launched in 2012, SalesFinder features daily deals on the best things to buy, see, eat, and drink from a variety of retailers across Ireland. You can also search for a range of products using our Search feature.

Our company philosophy is pretty simple: we treat our clients the way we like to be treated.

We built SalesFinder on the foundations below:

We only sell stuff we want to buy

Great prices are only half the battle - it’s also got to be a great product or service. Between our top-rated business partners and unbeatable prices, you should feel comfortable venturing out and trying something new!

No cheap talk

We are committed to giving you the full facts about all of our deals, without any sneaky or shrouded conditions that ruin the experience. That’s why all our fine print is clear and upfront on our website..

Dedicated customer service

We hate to leave clients and their customers hanging, and work hard to provide answers to all queries promptly. Please click here to get in touch with our team.
